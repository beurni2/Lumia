We need to fix idea repetition at the root level.

Even with narrative-shape diversity, ideas still feel identical because they are generated from the same underlying “idea DNA” (avoidance / low-energy contradiction).

We are adding TWO new generation axes:

1) IDEA ARCHETYPE (120 total)
2) SCENARIO EXPANSION (80–100 total)

These must be FIRST-CLASS inputs to generation (not post-labels).

PART 3 — GENERATION FLOW (MANDATORY)
--------------------------------------------------

REWRITE generator pipeline:

OLD:
scenario → template → hook

NEW:
1. pick archetype_family
2. pick archetype
3. pick scenario
4. pick narrative_shape
5. generate idea FROM archetype

Important:
- archetype must drive the idea
- not retrofitted after generation

--------------------------------------------------
PART 4 — HARD CONSTRAINTS
--------------------------------------------------

Batch of 3:

- 3 DIFFERENT archetypes (hard)
- 3 DIFFERENT families (hard)
- 3 DIFFERENT scenarios (already enforced)

Cross-batch memory:

- same archetype → -4 penalty
- same family → -2 penalty
- unseen archetype → +3 boost

Regenerate:

- must include ≥1 archetype from a family NOT in previous batch

- whatToShow
