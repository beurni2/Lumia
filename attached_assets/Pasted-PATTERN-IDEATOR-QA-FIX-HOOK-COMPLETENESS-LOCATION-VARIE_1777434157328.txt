PATTERN IDEATOR QA FIX — HOOK COMPLETENESS + LOCATION VARIETY

The latest output is better than before, but there are still three visible issues:

1. Too many hooks start the same:
- “the way I…”
- “me saying…”
- “I really…”

2. Some hook titles are unfinished / grammatically broken:
- “me saying I’ll go to the gym vs me the”
- “I really said I’d fold the laundry today and wearing”
- “the way I said I’d clear my inbox and marking”

3. Scenes/settings repeat too much:
- kitchen
- couch/bed
- same deadpan setup
- same “phone propped at chest height”
- same “single take, no edits” language

This makes the system feel templated even when topics differ.

---

FIX 1 — ADD HOOK COMPLETENESS VALIDATOR

Before any idea can ship, validate hook text.

Reject hook if:
- ends with “and”, “vs”, “but”, “with”, “to”, “the”, “my”, “me”
- contains broken phrase like “vs me the”
- has unresolved template fragments
- has mismatched grammar
- is missing the payoff half of a contrast
- feels like a clause, not a complete hook

Examples to reject:
- “me saying I’ll go to the gym vs me the”
- “I really said I’d fold the laundry today and wearing”
- “the way I said I’d clear my inbox and marking”

Examples to allow:
- “me saying I’ll go to the gym vs me still in bed”
- “I really said I’d fold laundry and wore the wrinkled shirt”
- “the way I said I’d clear my inbox and marked everything read”

If a hook fails:
- attempt deterministic repair once
- if repair fails, reject candidate

---

FIX 2 — REDUCE STARTER REPETITION

Within a batch:
- max 1 hook may start with “the way I”
- max 1 hook may start with “me saying”
- max 1 hook may start with “I really”
- max 1 hook may use “why did I”
- max 1 hook may use contrast syntax

Across regenerate history:
- penalize same hook opener heavily if used in previous batch

Add hookOpener field:
- the_way_i
- me_saying
- i_really
- why_did_i
- what_i_planned_vs
- this_is_where
- silent_panic
- realization
- denial_statement

Selection must diversify hookOpener, not just hookStyle.

---

Current outputs overuse:

Add and rotate settings:

Across regenerate:

---

FIX 4 — DIVERSIFY HOW TO FILM LANGUAGE

Current howToFilm repeats:
“Phone propped at chest height… single take, no edits. Natural light…”

This makes every idea feel identical.

Create filming instruction variants by visualActionPattern.

Examples:

fridge_open_stare:
“Set the phone on the counter. Open the fridge, pause, close it slowly, then look at camera.”

phone_scroll_freeze:
“Hold the phone in frame. Scroll once, freeze, then let the reaction sit.”

outfit_check_cut:
“Film in mirror. Look at the outfit, pause, then do the tiny ‘this is fine’ nod.”

work_fake_smile:
“Set phone near laptop. Read the message, fake-smile, then look back at screen.”

laundry_avoidance:
“Film near the laundry pile. Pick one item, inspect it, decide it’s wearable.”

social_cancel_relief:
“Film from the couch. Type the message, hit send, then relax like a weight left your body.”

No generic universal how-to unless needed.

---

FIX 5 — SCENE-SPECIFIC WHAT TO SHOW

Avoid generic blocks like:
“Open on you in the kitchen... Beat. Then the reaction lands...”

Each scenario should have a specific physical moment.

Bad:
“Open on you in the bed. Beat. Then the reaction lands...”

Good:
“You glance at the packed gym bag by the door, slowly pull the blanket higher, and look away like you never saw it.”

Bad:
“Open on you in the kitchen. stares into the fridge...”

Good:
“You open the fridge, stare at the same leftovers, close it, then immediately unlock DoorDash.”

---

FIX 6 — FINAL QA GATE

Before returning ideas, assert:

Per batch:
- no unfinished hook strings
- no duplicate hook opener
- no duplicate setting
- no duplicate visualActionPattern
- at least 2 different formats if possible
- howToFilm instructions are not identical templates
- whatToFilm has scenario-specific physical action

If gate fails:
- reselect or fallback
- do not ship broken hooks

Run 5 regenerate batches.
