PHASE 6 — BIG PREMISE + COMEDY COMPRESSION LAYER

Goal:
Upgrade ideas from small relatable moments → big, funny, viral premises.

Output must be:
- simple
- short
- immediately funny
- high emotional punch

---

PART 1 — BIG PREMISE TRANSFORMATION

After ideaCoreType + scenario are selected:

Transform idea into a stronger premise using:

1. SELF-ROAST
- exaggerate personal failure
- make creator the joke

Example:
"i avoided my to-do list"
→ "i ghosted my own to-do list"

---

2. ABSURD METAPHOR

- turn situation into something bigger/unexpected

Example:
"late night thoughts"
→ "my brain filed for emotional bankruptcy"

---

3. CONTRAST / DUALITY

- two opposing identities

Example:
"night vs morning"
→ "2AM me is a genius, 9AM me is a criminal"

---

4. OVER-DRAMATIZATION

- make small moment feel huge

Example:
"small mistake"
→ "this is where my life collapsed"

---

5. IDENTITY FRAMING

- tie to “who I am”

Example:
"i procrastinate"
→ "i specialize in disappointing my future self"

---

PART 2 — COMEDY COMPRESSION

After premise is generated:

Rules:

- ≤ 10 words (prefer 6–9)
- 1 clear idea
- no filler words
- no explanation
- no generic phrasing

---

PART 3 — ANTI-BORING RULES

Reject hook if:

- reads like observation instead of joke
- feels safe or neutral
- no emotional spike
- could apply to any scenario

---

PART 4 — FUNNINESS HEURISTICS

Boost if:

- unexpected phrasing
- contrast inside sentence
- self-roast tone
- slightly absurd but relatable

Penalty if:

- generic
- predictable
- flat tone
- “i did X again” type phrasing

---

PART 5 — KEEP SIMPLICITY

Do NOT:

- make it complex
- add multiple ideas
- over-explain
- make it “smart”

Goal:

simple + funny > clever

---

PART 6 — EXAMPLES (TARGET STYLE)

GOOD:

- "i ghosted my own to-do list"
- "my brain hates me after 11pm"
- "i gently disappoint my dreams weekly"
- "this is why i can’t have nice habits"
- "i am my own worst project"

BAD:

- "i tried to be productive but failed"
- "i struggle with motivation sometimes"
- "this happens to me often"
- "i was thinking about it again"

---

PART 7 — INTEGRATION

Flow becomes:

ideaCoreType
→ scenario
→ HookIntent
→ Big Premise transform
→ Comedy compression
→ template (only if needed)
→ pattern layer
→ final output

---

IMPORTANT

Do NOT:
- change Phase 5
- change HookIntent system
- change scoring system yet

This layer sits ABOVE templates, not replacing them.

---

SUCCESS CRITERIA

When reading hooks:

User reaction should be:
"this is funny immediately"

NOT:
"this is relatable but boring"
