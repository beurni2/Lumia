PHASE UI — SINGLE IDEA SWIPE EXPERIENCE

Goal:
Replace the current multi-idea display with a single-idea swipe-based feed that feels like a content experience, not a generator.

Each idea should feel like a post, not an option.

---

PART 1 — SCREEN STRUCTURE

Create a new screen:

IdeaFeedScreen

Component hierarchy:

<IdeaFeedScreen>
  <FeedHeader />
  <SwipeIdeaCard />
  <ReactionBar />
  <SwipeHints />
  <SecondaryActions />
  <BottomNav />
</IdeaFeedScreen>

---

PART 2 — FEED HEADER

Top section:

- Title: “3 ideas for today”
- Subtitle: “Made for your style”
- Settings icon (top-right)

Style:
- large title (32–40px)
- subtle subtitle (16–18px)
- left-aligned
- minimal UI

---

PART 3 — SWIPE IDEA CARD (CORE)

Create:

<SwipeIdeaCard />

This is the main focus.

Structure:

<IdeaCard>
  <IdeaMeta />
  <HeroHook />
  <ViralFeelBadge />
  <PatternPreview />
  <WhyItWorks />
</IdeaCard>

---

PART 3A — IDEA META

Top row inside card:

- “IDEA 1” (small, accent color)
- pattern or type pill (right side)

Keep minimal.

---

PART 3B — HERO HOOK (MOST IMPORTANT)

Display the hook text.

Rules:
- font size: 42–56px (mobile)
- bold
- max 3 lines
- center-left aligned
- occupies ~60–70% of visual focus

Example:
“i ghosted my own
to-do list”

---

PART 3C — VIRAL FEEL BADGE

Display a non-numeric signal:

Examples:
- “🔥 this would go viral”
- “⚡ high share potential”
- or dot indicator (●●●●○)

DO NOT show raw numbers.

---

PART 3D — PATTERN PREVIEW

Show filming hint:

Example:
“🎬 Silent Reaction”
“Look → pause → close app”

Keep short (1–2 lines max).

---

PART 3E — WHY IT WORKS (SHORT)

One-line explanation:

Example:
“Self-roast + everyday avoidance”

No long paragraphs.

---

PART 4 — SWIPE INTERACTIONS

Enable gestures:

- Swipe UP → next idea
- Swipe RIGHT → save idea
- Swipe LEFT → skip idea

Animations:
- smooth vertical card transition
- right swipe → card flies into saved
- left swipe → dismiss

Preload next 2 ideas in memory.

---

PART 5 — REACTION BAR

Below the card:

Buttons:

- “this is me”
- “too real”
- “would post”
- “meh”

Layout:
2x2 grid or horizontal row

Behavior:
- tap → triggers animation
- stores reaction
- optionally auto-advance after ~400ms

---

PART 6 — SECONDARY ACTIONS

Below reactions:

- Remix
- Copy
- More

Lower visual weight than reactions.

---

PART 7 — EXPANDED DETAIL (MODAL OR SHEET)

On tap “More” or card:

Show:

- Hook
- What to show
- How to film
- Caption
- Why it works

Keep feed clean; details optional.

---

PART 8 — BOTTOM NAV

Simple navigation:

- Ideas
- Studio
- Saved
- Profile

Minimal styling.

---

PART 9 — UX RULES

- Dark mode default
- Minimal UI (no clutter)
- No “AI-generated” labels
- Fast interactions (no loading feel)
- Preload next ideas

---

PART 10 — DATA INTEGRATION

Each idea object should include:

- hook (string)
- videoPattern (string)
- filmingGuide (array)
- viralFeelScore (0–10, not shown raw)
- premiseStyleId
- ideaCoreType

---

PART 11 — PERFORMANCE

- preload next 2 ideas
- no visible loading spinner between swipes
- cache last seen ideas for session continuity

---

PART 12 — SUCCESS CRITERIA

- user focuses on 1 idea at a time
- higher engagement per idea
- faster emotional reaction (not analysis)
- feels like browsing content, not selecting options
