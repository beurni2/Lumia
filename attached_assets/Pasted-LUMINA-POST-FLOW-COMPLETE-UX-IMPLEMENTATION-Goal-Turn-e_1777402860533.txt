LUMINA POST FLOW — COMPLETE UX IMPLEMENTATION

Goal:
Turn "export video" into "user actually posts" with minimal friction.
Flow must feel fast, guided, and action-oriented (no analysis, no thinking).

---

========================
1. POST-READY SCREEN
========================

HEADER:
"Your video is ready 🎉"
Subtext:
"Looks good. Now go post it."

---

VIDEO PREVIEW:
- Full-width video player
- Center play button
- Show duration (e.g. 00:00 / 00:18)
- NO before/after comparison

---

CONFIDENCE STRIP (3 items max):
- "Hook hits immediately"
- "Clear reaction moment"
- "Easy to watch and relatable"

Horizontal layout with icons.

---

========================
2. QUICK BOOST (OPTIONAL)
========================

Section title:
"Quick boost (optional)"

Subtext:
"2 taps max — makes this post hit harder"

Actions (max 2):

1. "Smoother flow"
   Button: "Fix"

2. "Faster hook"
   Button: "Fix"

---

INTERACTION:

On "Fix" tap:
- Light haptic feedback
- Button animates (scale down → up)
- Label → "Done ✓"
- Show micro text:
   "Clips feel more natural now"
   OR
   "Hook now hits instantly"
- Brief row glow (200–300ms)
- No loading state
- No screen refresh

---

========================
3. WATERMARK
========================

Label:
"Add 'Made with Lumina'"

Subtext:
"Support Lumina and get inspired ✨"

Toggle only. No technical explanation.

---

========================
4. PRIMARY CTA
========================

Button:
"Save & Post"

Subtext:
"Save to gallery and post anywhere"

Behavior:
- Save video locally
- Immediately transition to share flow

---

SECONDARY OPTION (IMPORTANT):

Below CTA (low emphasis):
"Just save"

Behavior:
- Save video only
- Show:
  "Saved ✓"
  "Post it later — it’s ready"

---

SECONDARY ACTIONS:
- "Make another version"
- "Back to ideas"

---

========================
5. SAVE SUCCESS STATE
========================

After "Save & Post":

Show:

"Saved ✓"
"Ready to post"
"Takes ~10 seconds"

Light animation + haptic

Then immediately open platform sheet

---

========================
6. PLATFORM SHEET
========================

Title:
"Post to"

Options:
- TikTok
- Instagram
- Snapchat
- Copy only

Large tap targets

---

========================
7. PLATFORM HANDOFF
========================

After platform selection:

Example (TikTok):

"Almost there!"
"Just 2 quick steps"

1. Tap "+"
2. Upload your video

---

========================
8. CAPTION FLOW (CRITICAL)
========================

Show:

"Your caption is ready"

Display generated caption

Buttons:
- "Copy caption"
- "Copy & open TikTok"

Optional:
Auto-copy caption on platform tap

---

========================
9. APP HANDOFF
========================

Attempt deep link to selected app

Fallback:
Keep instructions visible

---

========================
10. RETURN LOOP
========================

When user comes back to Lumina:

Show:

"Posted?"
"How did it go?"

Primary:
"Make another version"

Secondary:
"Back to ideas"

Optional:
"View saved videos"

---

========================
UX PRINCIPLES
========================

- No thinking required
- No long explanations
- No analysis after export
- Everything is action-driven

User should feel:

"I made something → this is ready → I posted it"

NOT:

"I need to review or figure this out"
