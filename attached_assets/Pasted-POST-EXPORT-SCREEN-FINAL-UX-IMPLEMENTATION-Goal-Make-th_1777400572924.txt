POST-EXPORT SCREEN — FINAL UX IMPLEMENTATION

Goal:
Make the post-export screen feel like a finish line and push immediate posting,
while allowing quick 1-tap improvements without friction.

---

1. HEADER

Title:
"Your video is ready 🎉"

Subtext:
"Looks good. Now go post it."

---

2. VIDEO PREVIEW

- Show final video (full width)
- Center play button overlay
- Show duration (e.g. 00:00 / 00:18)
- No before/after comparison

---

3. CONFIDENCE STRIP (3 ITEMS MAX)

Display 3 short confidence signals:

- "Hook hits immediately"
- "Clear reaction moment"
- "Easy to watch and relatable"

Style:
- icon + short text
- horizontal layout

---

4. QUICK BOOST SECTION

Rename section:

"Quick boost (optional)"

Remove all technical explanations.

Subtext:
"2 taps max — makes this post hit harder"

5. BOOST ACTIONS (MAX 2)

Action 1:
Label: "Smoother flow"
Button: "Fix"

Action 2:
Label: "Faster hook"
Button: "Fix"

On tap "Fix":

- Trigger light haptic feedback

- Change label → "Done ✓"
- Show micro confirmation text under row:

"Smoother flow" → "Clips feel more natural now"
"Faster hook" → "Hook now hits instantly"

- Brief row highlight (subtle glow, 200–300ms)

- DO NOT reload screen

"Add 'Made with Lumina'"

"Save & Post"

"Save to gallery and post anywhere"

9. SECONDARY ACTIONS

- "Make another version"

10. REMOVE ALL FRICTION

- "Saving works only on phone..."

UX PRINCIPLES
