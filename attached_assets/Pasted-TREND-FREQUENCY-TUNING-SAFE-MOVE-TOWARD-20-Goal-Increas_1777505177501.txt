TREND FREQUENCY TUNING — SAFE MOVE TOWARD ~20%

Goal:
Increase visible trend usage from ~12% toward ~20% WITHOUT forcing weak pairings.

Do NOT hard-force trends.
Do NOT inject trends just to hit a quota.
Quality > frequency.

---

1. TARGET RANGE

Trend usage target:
15–25%

This is a soft target, not a hard rule.

If no strong fit exists:
skip trend.

---

2. KEEP STRICT PRIMARY FIT

Primary trend injection still requires:
- compatible archetype / archetype family
- no avoidArchetype conflict
- region compatibility
- trend improves specificity
- trend does not feel forced

---

3. ADD SECONDARY FIT

Extend TrendItem:

secondaryFitArchetypes?: string[]
secondaryFitFamilies?: string[]

Use secondary fit ONLY if:
- no primary fit is available
- fit confidence remains above threshold

Secondary fit is lower priority than primary fit.

---

4. TREND FIT SCORE

Create trendFitScore from 0–10:

+4 primary archetype match
+3 primary family match
+2 secondary archetype match
+1 secondary family match
+1 region match or global
+1 high freshness

Penalties:
-5 avoidArchetype / avoidFamily match
-3 weak scenario fit
-3 trend feels decorative only
-2 niche/meme-dependent
-2 repeated trend from recent batch

Thresholds:
- score >= 6 → allowed
- score < 6 → skip

---

5. TRY MORE CANDIDATES

When considering trend injection:
- evaluate 2–3 candidate trends
- pick best scoring fit
- max 1 trend per idea

Do NOT use first matching trend blindly.

---

6. BAD PAIRING FIX

Tighten “girl dinner”:

Allowed:
- petty_logic
- self_deception
- low_energy_realism
- weird_habits

Avoid:
- productivity
- to_do_list
- planning
- work/task scenarios

Specifically prevent:
to-do list + girl dinner

---

7. BATCH CAP

Keep current hard cap:
- never all ideas trended
- default 0–1 trended ideas per batch of 3
- max 2 only if both are strong fits

---

8. QA

Run 10 batches.

Check:
- trend usage lands roughly 15–25%
- no forced pairings
- girl dinner no longer attaches to to-do/productivity
- captions remain human
- non-trend ideas still dominate
- no region leakage

Success:
Trends feel slightly more present, but still natural.
