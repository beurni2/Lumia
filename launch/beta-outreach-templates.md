## Channel cadence rules

| Channel | Daily cap | Wait before follow-up | Follow-up policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| IG / TikTok DM | 20 sends | 4 days | one soft bump, then drop |
| Reddit DM | 10 sends | 5 days | no bump unless they engage |
| Reddit comment | 5 / day | n/a | never bump in thread |
| Discord DM | 10 sends | 4 days | one soft bump, then drop |
| Email | 15 sends | 3 days | one soft bump, then drop |
| X DM | 15 sends | 3 days | one soft bump, then drop |

**Hard rules:**
- Personalize line 1 every time. A generic line 1 = the whole funnel dies.
- Never include the offer in the cold message — only after they reply.
- Log every send in the tracker within 5 minutes.
- Stop the send list immediately if reply rate falls below 8% over any rolling 25 sends.
